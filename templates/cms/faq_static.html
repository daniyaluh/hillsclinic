General Questions

What is limb lengthening surgery?

Limb lengthening surgery is a procedure that gradually increases the length of bones in the legs or arms. The surgery involves cutting the bone and using a device to slowly separate the bone ends, allowing new bone to form in the gap.

Am I a candidate for limb lengthening?

Good candidates are typically adults aged 18-50 in good overall health. We evaluate bone health, medical history, and psychological readiness. A consultation with our team will determine your suitability for the procedure.

Procedures

What methods do you offer?

We offer three main methods: Ilizarov (external frame), LON (combined internal nail + external frame), and PRECICE (fully internal magnetic nail). Each has different benefits, costs, and recovery times.

How much height can I gain?

Typical gains are 2-3 inches (5-8 cm) per bone segment. Femur (thigh) and tibia (shin) can each be lengthened. Combined procedures can achieve 4-6 inches total, done in stages for safety.

Cost & Payment

How much does limb lengthening cost?

Prices range from $3,000-$12,000 depending on the method chosen. Ilizarov is the most affordable ($3,000-4,000), LON is mid-range ($4,500-5,500), and PRECICE is premium ($8,000-12,000). All packages include surgery, hospital stay, and follow-up care.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options. A deposit is required to secure your surgery date, with the balance due before the procedure. Full payment upfront receives a discount. Contact us for personalized payment arrangements.

Recovery

How long is recovery?

Total recovery takes 6-12 months. The lengthening phase is 2-3 months (1mm/day), followed by a consolidation phase of 3-6 months for bone to fully heal. Most patients return to normal activities within 12 months.

International Patients

Do you help with visa and travel?

Yes! We provide medical visa invitation letters, airport pickup, accommodation recommendations, and translation services. Our international patient coordinators will guide you through every step of the process.
