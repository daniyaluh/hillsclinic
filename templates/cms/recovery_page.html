Recovery Timeline

1

Hospital Stay (Days 1-7)

You'll remain in the hospital for monitoring and initial recovery. Our team will teach you how to care for your device and manage pain.

  • 24/7 nursing care
  • Pain management
  • Initial physiotherapy
2

Lengthening Phase (Weeks 2-12)

Daily lengthening of approximately 1mm. Regular check-ups and X-rays to monitor progress. Daily physiotherapy is essential.

  • 1mm lengthening per day
  • Weekly X-ray monitoring
  • Daily physiotherapy exercises
3

Consolidation Phase (Months 3-9)

The new bone hardens and strengthens. Gradual increase in weight bearing. Frame/nail removal at the end of this phase.

  • Bone consolidation
  • Increased mobility
  • Hardware removal
4

Full Recovery (Months 9-12+)

Return to normal activities, sports, and full function. Final results are visible and you can enjoy your new height!

  • Return to sports
  • Full weight bearing
  • Enjoy your results!

Physiotherapy

Daily exercises are crucial for maintaining mobility and preventing complications. We provide a comprehensive PT program.

Nutrition

A protein-rich diet with adequate calcium and vitamin D supports bone healing. We provide dietary guidelines.

Pain Management

Discomfort is normal and manageable. We provide medications and techniques to keep you comfortable throughout recovery.

Wound Care

Proper pin site care prevents infection. We teach you exactly how to clean and dress your sites daily.

Mental Wellness

Recovery is a journey. Our support team is here to help you stay positive and motivated throughout the process.

Regular Check-ups

Weekly X-rays and check-ups ensure your progress is on track. Remote consultations available for international patients.
